About us

In addition to this, you will need to know more about it.Frédéric, in his thirties, is a young entrepreneur who has always dreamed of running his own business.For many years,he has been recognized in the family for making excellent Parmesan fondues. It was during a discussion with his father-in-law Daniel, who never stopped praising the exquisite taste of his fondues, that they decided to share the experience and market the product. Much to his delight, Daniel was thus securing his own supply of Parmesan fondue.

Frédéric has accumulated more than fifteen years of experience in the restaurant industry and he holds the necessary government certifications for the manufacture and sale of food products (MAPAQ).
